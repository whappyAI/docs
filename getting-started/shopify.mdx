# Shopify Setup Guide ðŸ›ï¸

Welcome to the **Laxi.ai Shopify integration guide**! This step-by-step tutorial will help you install and configure your **AI chatbot** for seamless ecommerce automation.

> **Prerequisite:** Ensure you have a **Shopify store** and admin access before proceeding.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install Laxi.ai from Shopify App Store">
    1. From your Shopify admin, navigate to **Apps > Visit Shopify App Store**  
    2. Install **[Laxi.ai](https://apps.shopify.com/laxi-ai)** and grant required permissions during setup  

    <img src="/images/getting-started/shopify-app-install.png" alt="Install Laxi.ai from Shopify" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '100%' }} />

    <Info>
    **Why Permissions Are Needed?** Laxi.ai requires access to **your product catalog & order data** to provide **intelligent product recommendations and AI-driven support**.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Login to Laxi.ai">
    After installation, you'll find **Laxi.ai** listed under the 'Apps' category in your Shopify admin.

    1. Click **Login to Laxi.ai** to access the **Laxi.ai portal**  
    2. This will open the **Laxi.ai dashboard**, where you can configure your chatbot  

    <img src="/images/getting-started/login-laxi.png" alt="Login to Laxi.ai Portal" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '100%' }} />

    > Learn more about portal access [here](../agent-config/portal-access)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Agent Presence">
    Next, ensure your **AI chatbot is available** on your Shopify store.

    1. In the **Laxi.ai dashboard**, go to **Agent Settings**  
    2. Confirm your agent is **updated** and ready for integration  

    Click **Setup Agent** to go directly to configuration settings.

    <img src="/images/getting-started/agent-presence.png" alt="Agent Presence Setup" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '100%' }} />

    > Learn more about agent configuration [here](../agent-config/agent-configuration)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Finalize Your Design">
    Customize the chatbot's **appearance and user experience** to match your store.

    1. **Set chatbot color scheme**  
    2. **Adjust widget size & position**  
    3. **Define welcome messages & AI tone**  

    Click **Open Design Section**to go directly to design settings.

    <img src="/images/getting-started/finalize-design.png" alt="Chatbot Design Settings" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '100%' }} />

    > Learn more about design customization [here](../agent-config/design-customizations)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update Data Sources">
    Ensure your chatbot **has access to your storeâ€™s products and knowledge base**.

    1. Open **Laxi.ai Dashboard â†’ Data Sources**  
    2. Confirm **Shopify sync is active** (your product count should be visible)  
    3. Optionally, add more data (PDFs, FAQs, Google Sheets)  

    Click **Access Data Sources** to go directly to data sources settings.

    <img src="/images/getting-started/update-data-sources.png" alt="Update Data Sources" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '100%' }} />

    > Learn more about data management [here](../agent-config/data-sources)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Activate Laxi.ai Widget">
    This step **embeds the chatbot** into your Shopify storefront.

    1. **Quick Activation:**  
       - Option A: In Shopify Admin, go to **Online Store â†’ Themes â†’ Customize Theme â†’ App Embeds**  
       - Option B: Click the **Enable Widget** buttom

    <img src="/images/getting-started/activate-app-embed.png" alt="Enable Laxi.ai Widget" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '100%' }} />

    2. **Toggle** `Laxi.ai Widget` to active position  
    3. **Confirm changes** with save button (critical for activation)

    <img src="/images/getting-started/activate-shopify.png" alt="Click Save (Theme Settings)" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '100%' }} />
  </Step>
</Steps>
