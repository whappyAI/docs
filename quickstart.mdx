# Getting Started with Whappy

Whappy helps you automate WhatsApp conversations to qualify leads and book appointments. This guide walks you through setting up your first campaign, from initial context to managing qualified leads.

---

## 1. Create Your First Campaign

Start by navigating to **Dashboard â†’ Campaign** to set up your WhatsApp automation campaign.

### Set Your Context
Define the foundation of your AI assistant:

- **WhatsApp Name**: The name your AI will use when messaging leads
- **Business Activity**: Describe what your business does
- **Conversation Objective**: What you want to achieve (qualify leads, book demos, etc.)
- **Additional Information**: Any extra context the AI should know

<img src="/images/campaign/context-setup.png" alt="Setting up campaign context" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '100%' }} />

<Tip>
    Be specific about your business and objectives. The AI uses this information to have natural, relevant conversations with your leads.
</Tip>

---

## 2. Define Lead Information Fields

Navigate to the **Info** tab to specify what information you want to collect from leads during your WhatsApp conversations.

<img src="/images/campaign/info-fields.png" alt="Defining lead information fields" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '100%' }} />

**Example Fields:**
- Budget range (Multiple choice)
- Project timeline (Text input)
- Company size (Number)

---

## 3. Build Your Conversation Funnel

In the **Funnel** tab, create the conversation flow using drag-and-drop nodes:

Build your conversation flow using simple drag-and-drop nodes to guide leads through qualification and booking.

<img src="/images/campaign/funnel-builder.png" alt="Building conversation funnel" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '100%' }} />

<Note>
    Use the Test button to simulate conversations and refine your funnel before going live.
</Note>

---

## 4. Configure Campaign Options

Set your **operating hours** and **timezone** in the **Options** tab:

- **Timezone**: Select your business timezone
- **Operating Days**: Choose which days to run campaigns
- **Active Hours**: Set start and end times for each day

<img src="/images/campaign/options-schedule.png" alt="Configuring campaign schedule" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '100%' }} />

---

## 5. Set Up WhatsApp Integration

Navigate to **Integrations â†’ WhatsApp** to connect your WhatsApp Business account and configure message templates.

<img src="/images/integrations/whatsapp-setup.png" alt="WhatsApp integration setup" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '100%' }} />

<Warning>
    WhatsApp templates must be approved before you can send messages. Set up your integration early in the process.
</Warning>

---

## 6. Test Your Campaign

Before going live:

1. **Test the Funnel**: Use the Test button in the funnel builder
2. **Verify Integrations**: Check WhatsApp connection status
3. **Review Message Templates**: Ensure they're approved and working

<img src="/images/campaign/test-conversation.png" alt="Testing campaign conversation" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '70%' }} />

---

## 7. Launch and Manage Leads

### Start Your Campaign
Once everything is configured and tested:
1. Go back to your **Campaign** page
2. Click the **Start Campaign** button
3. Your campaign status will show as "Running"

### Add Leads to Your Campaign
Navigate to **Leads** to add contacts to your campaign:

- **Manual Entry**: Add leads individually
- **Import**: Upload lead lists
- **Automation**: Connect with Zapier or other tools

<img src="/images/leads/lead-management.png" alt="Managing leads dashboard" style={{ marginTop: '1rem', maxWidth: '100%' }} />

### Monitor Conversations
Track lead interactions in real-time:
- View conversation history
- See qualification status
- Check appointment bookings
- Export qualified leads
